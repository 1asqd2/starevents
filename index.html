About Us

Established in 2007, Stars Events started with the supply of concert equipment. We soon expanded to work in lighting and theater decorations and eventually branched out to providing photography and video services.

Today the company is equipped to setup a complete range of events from Large Festivals to Weddings and meetings with a fleet of trucks and highly skilled technicians and engineers alongside a top of the line warehouse of sound, light, staging devices and LED screens.

We are a full-service events company with an international outlook and strong regional presence, making us one of the leading event management companies in UAE.
